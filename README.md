# FUTURE_DS_01 - Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Objective
Analyze Superstore sales data to identify revenue trends,
top-selling products, and regional performance.

## Tools Used
- Python (Google Colab)
- Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Insights
- Total Revenue: $2,297,200.86
- Total Profit: $286,397.02
- Profit Margin: 12.47%
- Best Region: West ($108,418.45)
- Worst Region: Central ($39,706.36)
- Highest Margin Category: Technology (17.40%)
- Lowest Margin Category: Furniture (2.49%)

## Top Loss-Making Products
- Cubify CubeX 3D Printer Double Head: -$8,879.97
- Lexmark MX611dhe Laser Printer: -$4,589.97
- Cubify CubeX Triple Head Print: -$3,839.99

## Recommendations
1. Focus budget on West & East regions
2. Review Furniture category pricing
3. Discontinue or reprice loss-making products
4. Reduce heavy discounts — they reduce profit
5. Expand Technology product range (best ROI)
